About Calorie Calculation

Your daily calorie target is calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, which is considered more accurate than the Harris-Benedict equation.

Formula:

  • Men: BMR = 10 × weight(kg) + 6.25 × height(cm) - 5 × age(years) + 5
  • Women: BMR = 10 × weight(kg) + 6.25 × height(cm) - 5 × age(years) - 161

The BMR is then multiplied by your activity level to get your daily calorie needs.
